Wisconsin Nature Resources

Here's a collection of resources that I've found useful for learning about nature in Wisconsin. There's a strong bias towards ecosystems and organisms found in Southern Wisconsin; these resources may or may not be useful for learning about Northern Wisconsin.

I am a Wisconsin Master Naturalist. I hope that these resources may be useful to other Master Naturalists and to anyone else trying to learn more about nature.

The topics covered include:

  • birds
  • plants
  • conservation
